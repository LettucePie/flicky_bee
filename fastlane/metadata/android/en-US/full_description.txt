Play as a bee and casually travel from flower to flower, collecting honey and avoiding danger. You get tired over time, eventually returning to your hive with a score of Honey Points. Spend the Honey Points on various cosmetics to spice up your next run.

Made with:
- Godot 4
- LMMS
- Krita
- blender
- Audacity

Menu and Play songs by KenneyNL
